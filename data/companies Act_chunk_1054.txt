(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Publication of draft terms [F1183by registrar](merger) The directors of each of the merging companies must deliver a copy of the draft terms to the registrar. The registrar must publish in the Gazette notice of receipt by him from that company of a copy of the draft terms. That notice must be published at least one month before the date of any meeting of that company summoned for the purpose of approving the scheme.
