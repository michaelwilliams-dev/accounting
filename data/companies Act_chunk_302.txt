The copy of the resolution must be accompanied by a statement informing the member— how to signify agreement to the resolution (see section 296), and as to the date by which the resolution must be passed if it is not to lapse (see section 297). In the event of default in complying with this section, an offence is committed by every officer of the company who is in default. A person guilty of an offence under this section is liable— on conviction on indictment, to a fine; on summary conviction, to a fine not exceeding the statutory maximum.
