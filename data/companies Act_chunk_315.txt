In subsection (1), the reference to members includes any person who is entitled to a share in consequence of the death or bankruptcy of a member, if the company has been notified of their entitlement. In subsection (2), the reference to the bankruptcy of a member includes— the sequestration of the estate of a member; a member's estate being the subject of a protected trust deed (within the meaning of the Bankruptcy (Scotland) Act [F2402016]). This section has effect subject to— any enactment, and any provision of the company's articles. Contents of notices of meetings Notice of a general meeting of a company must state— the time and date of the meeting, and the place of the meeting. Notice of a general meeting of a company must state the general nature of the business to be dealt with at the meeting.
