(Schedule 1, paragraph 36(4)) This only applies in certain circumstances; for example, it does not apply to derivatives. It applies where investments in subsidiaries, associates and joint ventures are measured at fair value through profit or loss. When it applies, the disclosures required by Section 11 Basic Financial Instruments that relate to financial assets and financial liabilities measured at fair value, including paragraph 11.48A, shall be given.
