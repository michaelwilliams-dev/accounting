Subject to that, the Appeal Officer holds and vacates office on the terms determined by the Secretary of State. Remuneration and pensions (1) The Secretary of State may pay remuneration and travelling and other allowances to the Appeal Officer. The Secretary of State may— pay a pension, allowance or gratuity to or in respect of a person who is or has been the Appeal Officer, or make contributions or payments towards provision for a pension, allowance or gratuity for or in respect of such a person.
