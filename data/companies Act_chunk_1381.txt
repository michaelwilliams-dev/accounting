Where the consideration or part of it is valued by a person other than the valuer himself, the latter's report must state that fact and shall also— state the former's name and what knowledge and experience he has to carry out the valuation, and describe so much of the consideration as was valued by the other person, and the method used to value it, and specify the date of that valuation.
