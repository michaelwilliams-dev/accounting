Both current UK accounting standards and FRS 102 The Financial Reporting Standard applicable in the UK and Republic of Ireland require financial assets to be carried at the lower of their carrying amount or recoverable amount. A financial asset is therefore impaired when there is a reduction in its recoverable amount below its carrying amount.
