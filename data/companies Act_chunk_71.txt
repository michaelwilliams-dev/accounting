If a company fails, without reasonable excuse, to comply with this section an offence is committed by— the company, and every officer of the company who is in default. A person guilty of an offence under this section is liable on summary conviction— in England and Wales, to a fine; in Scotland or Northern Ireland, to a fine not exceeding level 5 on the standard scale and, for continued contravention, a daily default fine not exceeding one- tenth of level 5 on the standard scale.
