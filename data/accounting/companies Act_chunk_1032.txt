In this section “relevant creditor” means— a creditor in respect of a moratorium debt, or a creditor in respect of a priority pre-moratorium debt. The relevant creditors may not participate in the meeting summoned under section 896. For the purposes of section 897 (statement to be circulated or made available)— the requirement in section 897(1)(a) is to be read as including a requirement to send each relevant creditor a statement complying with section 897; any reference to creditors entitled to attend the meeting summoned under section 896 includes a reference to relevant creditors.
