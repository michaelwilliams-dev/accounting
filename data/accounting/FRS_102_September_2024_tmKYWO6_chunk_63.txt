Payments to third parties for services of directors (Sections 305A and 306 of the Companies Act 2014)25 1AD.40 The notes to the financial statements of a small entity shall disclose, both for the current and the preceding reporting period, the aggregate amount of any consideration paid to, or receivable by, third parties for making available the services of any person: as a director of the small entity; as director of any of its subsidiaries; or otherwise in connection with the management of the small entity’s affairs or any of its subsidiaries. This disclosure shall include all relevant sums paid by or receivable from, and shall distinguish between the sums respectively paid by, or receivable from, the small entity, its subsidiaries, any parent of the small entity and any other persons.
