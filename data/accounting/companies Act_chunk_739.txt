(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Chapter 8 Alteration of share capital How share capital may be altered Alteration of share capital of limited company A limited company having a share capital may not alter its share capital except in the following ways. The company may— increase its share capital by allotting new shares in accordance with this Part, or reduce its share capital in accordance with Chapter 10. The company may— sub-divide or consolidate all or any of its share capital in accordance with section 618, or reconvert stock into shares in accordance with section 620. The company may redenominate all or any of its shares in accordance with section 622, and may reduce its share capital in accordance with section 626 in connection with such a redenomination.
