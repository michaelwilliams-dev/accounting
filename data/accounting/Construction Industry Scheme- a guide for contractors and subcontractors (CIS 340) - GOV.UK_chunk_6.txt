Registering for the scheme — subcontractors When to register Register when you are, or your firm or company is, about to start work within the construction industry. How to register If you’re a sole trader, partnership or limited company and you already have a Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR), you can register for CIS online or register and apply for gross payment status. To use this service, you need a Government Gateway user ID and password. If you do not have a user ID, you can create one when you register. If you do not have a UTR, register as a new business for Self Assessment and choose ‘working as a subcontractor’ when prompted. You’ll be registered for Self Assessment and CIS at the same time. If you do not have a Limited Company UTR, register your company before registering as a CIS subcontractor. If you are based abroad You should still register for CIS if you’re a subcontractor based abroad but do construction work in the UK. What happens next HMRC will write and confirm your registration. We’ll tell you what information you need to give to your contractors before they pay you.
