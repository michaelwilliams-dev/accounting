Supplier finance arrangements B7.3 As part of the Periodic Review 2024 amendments, disclosure requirements were introduced in Section 7 based on the IASB’s May 2023 Supplier Finance Arrangements (Amendments to IAS 7 and IFRS 7). B7.4 The key difference from the IASB’s requirements is that Section 7 does not require disclosure of amounts settled by finance providers with suppliers. Respondents had mixed views on whether this requirement would be cost-beneficial so the FRC concluded on proportionality grounds that it should be omitted. B7.5 Prior to the Periodic Review 2024 amendments, qualifying entities were exempt from all the requirements of Section 7. However, in response to stakeholder feedback, qualifying entities are only exempt from the supplier finance arrangements disclosure requirements if equivalent disclosures are included in the consolidated financial statements.
