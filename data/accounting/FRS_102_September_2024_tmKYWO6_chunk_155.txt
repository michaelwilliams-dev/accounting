Section 11 Basic Financial Instruments Scope of Sections 11 and 12 This section and Section 12 Other Financial Instruments Issues together set out the requirements for the recognition, derecognition, measurement and disclosure of financial instruments (financial assets and financial liabilities). This section applies to basic financial instruments and is relevant to all entities. Section 12 applies to other, more complex financial instruments and transactions. If an entity enters into only basic financial instrument transactions then Section 12 is not applicable. However, even entities with only basic financial instruments shall consider the scope of Section 12 to ensure that it does not apply.
