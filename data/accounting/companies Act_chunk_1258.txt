(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes 1079 Effect of failure to give public notice A company is not entitled to rely against other persons on the happening of any event to which this section applies unless— the event has been officially notified at the material time, or the company shows that the person concerned knew of the event at the material time. The events to which this section applies are— an amendment of the company's articles, a change among the company's directors, (as regards service of any document on the company) a change of the company's registered office, the making of a winding-up order in respect of the company, or the appointment of a liquidator in a voluntary winding up of the company.
