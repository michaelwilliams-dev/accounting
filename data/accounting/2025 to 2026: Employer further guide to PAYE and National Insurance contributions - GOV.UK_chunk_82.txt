What do do when claiming national insurance contributions relief From April 2025, an eligible employer operating in a designated Freeport special tax site, and who wishes to claim the employer national insurance contributions relief, will be required to provide the workplace postcode for any qualifying employees within the RTI Full Payment Submission. Where you are eligible to claim this relief but there is no corresponding category letter, you must send a refund request for the relief to be repaid by HMRC.
