If a company fails to provide the information required under subsection (1), an offence is committed by— the company, and every officer of the company who is in default. A person guilty of an offence under this section is liable on summary conviction to a fine not exceeding level 3 on the standard scale. 790T Protected information Section 790N and subsections (1) and (2) of section 790O are subject to— section 790ZF (protection of information as to usual residential address), and any provision of regulations made under section 790ZG (protection of material). Subsection (1) is not to be taken to affect the generality of the power conferred by virtue of section 790ZG(3)(f).
