Regulations that— restrict the classes of company which have the benefit of any exemption, exception or special provision, require additional matter to be included in a document of any class, or otherwise render the requirements of this Part more onerous, are subject to affirmative resolution procedure. Otherwise, the regulations are subject to negative resolution procedure.
