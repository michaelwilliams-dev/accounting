The meeting must be called in the same manner, as nearly as possible, as that in which meetings are required to be called by directors of the company. The business which may be dealt with at the meeting includes a resolution of which notice is given in accordance with this section. Any reasonable expenses incurred by the members requesting the meeting by reason of the failure of the directors duly to call a meeting must be reimbursed by the company. Any sum so reimbursed shall be retained by the company out of any sums due or to become due from the company by way of fees or other remuneration in respect of the services of such of the directors as were in default.
