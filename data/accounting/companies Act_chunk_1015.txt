(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes The register 859I Entries on the register This section applies where a charge is registered in accordance with a provision of this Chapter. The registrar must— allocate to the charge a unique reference code and place a note in the register recording that reference code; and include in the register any documents delivered under section 859A(3) or (5), 859B(3), (4) or (7), or 859C(3). The registrar must give a certificate of the registration of the charge to the person who delivered to the registrar a section 859D statement of particulars relating to the charge. The certificate must state— the registered name and number of the company in respect of which the charge was registered; and the unique reference code allocated to the charge. The certificate must be signed by the registrar or authenticated by the registrar's official seal.
