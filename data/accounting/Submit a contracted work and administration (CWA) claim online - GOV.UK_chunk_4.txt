amount New matter starts for legal help ‘New matter starts’ is the LAA contracting term used for legal help, eg, the first meeting a provider has with a client. How to report new matter starts depends on what method of submission you’re using. LAA’s bulkload spreadsheet Enter new matter starts into the controlled matter start form (CMSF) area of the spreadsheet. CMS file Contact your software supplier to find out if your CMS bulkload file contains your new matter starts. Line-by-line entry Manually enter new matter starts at step 3 of the submission process. Remember, you’re updating the live system so only 1 person can do this at a time. Which matter type to use for civil outcomes The civil codes guidance (https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/consolidated-matter- report-forms-civil-codes) includes help on selecting matter types when reporting completed civil work.
