Approval by written resolution: accidental failure to send memorandum Where— approval under this Chapter is sought by written resolution, and a memorandum is required under this Chapter to be sent or submitted to every eligible member before the resolution is passed, any accidental failure to send or submit the memorandum to one or more members shall be disregarded for the purpose of determining whether the requirement has been met. Subsection (1) has effect subject to any provision of the company's articles. Cases where approval is required under more than one provision Approval may be required under more than one provision of this Chapter. If so, the requirements of each applicable provision must be met. This does not require a separate resolution for the purposes of each provision.
