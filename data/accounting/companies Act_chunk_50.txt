[F49(1A) Where a direction is given under subsection (1), the registrar may omit from the material on the register that is available for public inspection any mention of the name to which the direction relates (so far as it relates to the company to which the direction is given).] The Secretary of State may make provision by regulations supplementing this section. The regulations may make provision— as to matters that are to be disregarded, and as to words, expressions, signs or symbols that are, or are not, to be regarded as the same, for the purposes of this section. The regulations may provide— that no direction is to be given under this section in respect of a name— in specified circumstances, or if specified consent is given, and that a subsequent change of circumstances or withdrawal of consent does not give rise to grounds for a direction under this section. Regulations under this section are subject to negative resolution procedure. In this section “specified” means specified in the regulations.
