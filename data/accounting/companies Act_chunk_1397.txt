Any reference in the Companies Acts to the appointment of a receiver or manager under powers contained in an instrument includes an appointment made under powers that by virtue of an enactment are implied in and have effect as if contained in an instrument. 1170B Meaning of “contributory” In the Companies Acts “ contributory ” means every person liable to contribute to the assets of a company in the event of its being wound up. For the purposes of all proceedings for determining, and all proceedings prior to the final determination of, the persons who are to be deemed contributories, the expression includes any person alleged to be a contributory.
