For the purposes of this section, and regulations made under it, amounts paid to or receivable by— a person connected with a director, or a body corporate controlled by a director, are treated as paid to or receivable by the director. The expressions “connected with” and “controlled by” in this subsection have the same meaning as in Part 10 (company directors). It is the duty of— any director of a company, and any person who is or has at any time in the preceding five years been a director of the company, to give notice to the company of such matters relating to himself as may be necessary for the purposes of regulations under this section.
