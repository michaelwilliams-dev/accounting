The order must specify a date by which the respondent company's name is to be changed and may be enforced— in England and Wales or Northern Ireland, in the same way as an order of the High Court; in Scotland, in the same way as a decree of the Court of Session. If the respondent company's name is not changed in accordance with the order by the specified date, the adjudicator may determine a new name for the company.
