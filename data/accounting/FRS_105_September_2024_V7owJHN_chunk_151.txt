A4.3 [Deleted] Micro companies under Irish company law A4.4 The Companies Act 2014 includes a micro companies regime (which is similar but not identical to that in the UK). When a company qualifies as a micro company in accordance with section 280D of the Companies Act 2014, then the micro companies regime may be applied by the company in respect of financial statements and reports for a financial year, in relation to which that company qualifies as a micro company (section 280E of the Companies Act 2014). The definition of a micro company is contained in section 280D of the Companies Act 2014.
