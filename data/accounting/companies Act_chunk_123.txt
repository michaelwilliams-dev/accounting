128E Duty to notify registrar of changes The duty under subsection (2) applies during the period when an election under section 128B is in force. The company must deliver to the registrar any relevant information that the company would during that period have been obliged under this Act to enter in its register of members, had the election not been in force. “Relevant information” means information other than— the date mentioned in section 113(2)(b) (date when person registered as member), the date mentioned in section 123(3)(b) (date when membership of limited company increases from one to two or more members), and the dates mentioned in the following provisions, but only in cases where the date to be recorded in the central register is to be the date on which the document containing information of the relevant change is registered by the registrar— section 113(2)(c) (date when person ceases to be member), section 123(2)(b) (date when company becomes single member company).
