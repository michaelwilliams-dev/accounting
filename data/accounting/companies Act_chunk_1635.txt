(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes (1) Rights held by a person as nominee for another shall be treated as held by the other. Rights shall be regarded as held as nominee for another if they are exercisable only on his instructions or with his consent or concurrence. Rights attached to shares held by way of security Rights attached to shares held by way of security shall be treated as held by the person providing the security— where apart from the right to exercise them for the purpose of preserving the value of the security, or of realising it, the rights are exercisable only in accordance with his instructions, and where the shares are held in connection with the granting of loans as part of normal business activities and apart from the right to exercise them for the purpose of preserving the value of the security, or of realising it, the rights are exercisable only in his interests.
