(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Relationship between this Chapter and provisions of company's articles A provision of the articles of a private company is void in so far as it would have the effect that a resolution that is required by or otherwise provided for in an enactment could not be proposed and passed as a written resolution. Chapter 3 Resolutions at meetings General provisions about resolutions at meetings Resolutions at general meetings A resolution of the members of a company is validly passed at a general meeting if— notice of the meeting and of the resolution is given, and the meeting is held and conducted, in accordance with the provisions of this Chapter (and, where relevant, Chapter 4) and the company's articles. Calling meetings Directors' power to call general meetings The directors of a company may call a general meeting of the company. Members' power to require directors to call general meeting The members of a company may require the directors to call a general meeting of the company.
