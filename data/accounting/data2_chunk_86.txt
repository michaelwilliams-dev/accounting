SCHEDULE 3 Section 27 Regulator of Community Interest Companies Regulator’s terms of appointment (1) The period for which a person is appointed as Regulator must not exceed five years. A person who has held office as Regulator may be re-appointed, once only, for a further period not exceeding five years. The Regulator may at any time resign the office by giving notice in writing to the Secretary of State. The Secretary of State may at any time remove the Regulator on the ground of incapacity or misbehaviour. Subject to that, the Regulator holds and vacates office on the terms determined by the Secretary of State. Remuneration and pensions (1) The Secretary of State may pay remuneration and travelling and other allowances to the Regulator.
