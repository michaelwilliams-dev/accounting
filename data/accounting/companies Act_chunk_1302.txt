(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes 1097 Powers of court on ordering removal of material from the register Where the court makes an order for the removal of anything from the register under section 1096 (rectification of the register), it may give directions under this section. It may direct that any note on the register that is related to the material that is the subject of the court's order shall be removed from the register. It may direct that its order shall not be available for public inspection as part of the register.
