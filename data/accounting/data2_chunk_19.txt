For the purposes of this section— “grant-aided body” means a body to whom the Secretary of State has paid, or is proposing to pay, grant under section 16; and any expenses of any body carrying on subsidiary activities of the grant-aided body (within the meaning of that section) are to be regarded as expenses of the grant-aided body.
