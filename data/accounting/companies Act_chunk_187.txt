Chapter 3 Declaration of interest in existing transaction or arrangement Declaration of interest in existing transaction or arrangement Where a director of a company is in any way, directly or indirectly, interested in a transaction or arrangement that has been entered into by the company, he must declare the nature and extent of the interest to the other directors in accordance with this section.
