CIS vouchers received during the year From 6 April 2002, companies who have deductions made from their income as subcontractors are able to set-oﬀ these deductions against the monthly or quarterly PAYE / NICs and any CIS liabilities due from their employees and / or subcontractors. It is possible you may receive vouchers during the year from companies who wish to take advantage of these procedures. You must only retain vouchers from companies who have ceased to trade.
