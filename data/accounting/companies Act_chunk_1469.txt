In this section “specified” means specified in, or of a description specified in, the suspension notice in question. 1235 Effect of suspension notices An Auditor General must not act as a statutory auditor at any time when a suspension notice issued to him in respect of the audited person has effect. If at any time during an Auditor General's term of office as a statutory auditor a suspension notice issued to him in respect of the audited person takes effect, he must immediately— resign his office (with immediate effect), and give notice in writing to the audited person that he has resigned by reason of his becoming ineligible for appointment.
