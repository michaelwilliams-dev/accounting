The Secretary of State may make provision by regulations supplementing this section. The regulations may make provision— as to matters that are to be disregarded, and as to words, expressions, signs or symbols that are, or are not, to be regarded as the same, for the purposes of this section. The regulations may provide— that registration by a name that would otherwise be prohibited under this section is permitted— in specified circumstances, or with specified consent, and that if those circumstances obtain or that consent is given at the time a company is registered by a name, a subsequent change of circumstances or withdrawal of consent does not affect the registration. Regulations under this section are subject to negative resolution procedure. In this section “specified” means specified in the regulations.
