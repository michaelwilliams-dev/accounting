Financial Conduct Authority. 9.3 Qualifying as a small company every year Generally, a company qualifies as small in its first financial year if it meets the conditions in that year. In any following years, a company must meet the conditions in that year and the year before. If a company qualified as small in one year, but no longer meets the criteria in the next year - it may continue to claim the exemptions available in the next year.
