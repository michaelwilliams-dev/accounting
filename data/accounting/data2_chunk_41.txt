The application— must be made within 28 days after the date on which the resolutions are passed or made (or, if the resolutions are passed or made on different days, the date on which the last of them is passed or made), and may be made on behalf of the persons entitled to make it by such one or more of their number as they may appoint for the purpose. On the hearing of the application the court shall make an order either cancelling or confirming the resolutions. The court may— make that order on such terms and conditions as it thinks fit, if it thinks fit adjourn the proceedings in order that an arrangement may be made to the satisfaction of the court for the purchase of the interests of dissentient members, and give such directions, and make such orders, as it thinks expedient for facilitating or carrying into effect any such arrangement.
