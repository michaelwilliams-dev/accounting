Whether the time allowed is that for a private company or a public company is determined by reference to the company's status immediately before the end of the accounting reference period by reference to which the financial year for the accounts in question was determined. In this section the “relevant accounts meeting” means the accounts meeting of the company at which the accounts and reports in question are to be laid. Default in sending out copies of accounts and reports: offences If default is made in complying with section 423 or 424, an offence is committed by— the company, and every officer of the company who is in default. A person guilty of an offence under this section is liable— on conviction on indictment, to a fine; on summary conviction, to a fine not exceeding the statutory maximum.
