A request— may be sent to the company in hard copy or electronic form, must identify the statement to which it relates, must be authenticated by the person or persons making it, and must be received by the company at least one week before the meeting to which it relates. A quoted company is not required to place on a website a statement under this section if, on an application by the company or another person who claims to be aggrieved, the court is satisfied that the rights conferred by this section are being abused. The court may order the members requesting website publication to pay the whole or part of the company's costs (in Scotland, expenses) on such an application, even if they are not parties to the application.
