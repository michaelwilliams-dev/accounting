A person guilty of an offence under this section is liable on summary conviction to a fine not exceeding level 3 on the standard scale.] Resolution requiring special notice Where by any provision of the Companies Acts special notice is required of a resolution, the resolution is not effective unless notice of the intention to move it has been given to the company at least 28 days before the meeting at which it is moved. The company must, where practicable, give its members notice of any such resolution in the same manner and at the same time as it gives notice of the meeting.
