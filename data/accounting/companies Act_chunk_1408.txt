The regulations may provide that a person subject to foreign restrictions— is disqualified automatically by virtue of the regulations, or may be disqualified by order of the court on the application of the Secretary of State. The regulations may provide for applications to the court by persons disqualified under this Part for permission to act in a way which would otherwise be in breach of the disqualification.
