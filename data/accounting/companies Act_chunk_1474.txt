Power to approve third countries as equivalent or transitional third countries The Secretary of State may by regulations grant to a third country, or make provision for the grant to a third country of— approval as an equivalent third country, provisional approval, for a period of up to seven years, as an equivalent third country, or transitional approval, for a period of up to seven years, as a transitional third country, in relation to the comparability of the third country’s audit regulatory regime to the audit regulatory regime of the United Kingdom.
