(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Supplementary 226E Payments made without approval: civil consequences An obligation (however arising) to make a payment which would be in contravention of section 226B or 226C has no effect. If a payment is made in contravention of section 226B or 226C— it is held by the recipient on trust for the company or other person making the payment, and in the case of a payment by a company, any director who authorised the payment is jointly and severally liable to indemnify the company that made the payment for any loss resulting from it.
