Guidance Register a partnership as a subcontractor with payment under deduction by post Use postal form CIS304 to register as a Construction Industry Scheme (CIS) subcontractor with payment under deduction. From: HM Revenue & Customs (/government/organisations/hm-revenue-customs) Published 27 August 2024 Last updated 26 February 2025 — Before you start The quickest way to register as a CIS subcontractor (https://www.gov.uk/what-you-must- do-as-a-cis-subcontractor/how-to-register) is online. You may also be able to apply for g ross payment status (https://www.gov.uk/what-you-must-do-as-a-cis-subcontractor/gross- payment-status) if you do not want deductions to be made in advance by contractors. Register a partnership You can find information on completing the CIS304 postal form in the partnership registration guidance notes (https://assets.publishing.service.gov.uk/media/67b5ddf1ae5d020b0914bb99/cis304-register- a-partnership-as-a-subcontractor-notes.pdf) (PDF, 489 KB, 5 pages). Get all of your information together before you start. You will fill this form in online and you cannot save your progress. Fill in CIS304 form (https://www.tax.service.gov.uk/print-and- post/form/SpecPersTax_A/1.0/CIS304/cis304.xdp). Print and post it to HMRC (the address is at the end of the form).
