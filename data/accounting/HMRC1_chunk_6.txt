A charity is a limited company if it was set up by a: constitution memorandum and articles of association (/limited-company- formation/memorandum-and-articles-of-association) royal charter or Act of Parliament Limited companies must also send annual accounts (/prepare-file-annual-accounts- for-limited-company/overview) to Companies House. Trusts Complete a Trust and Estate Self Assessment tax return (/self-assessment-forms- and-helpsheets) if your charity is a trust. A charity is a trust if it was set up by a trust deed or will. Penalties and deadlines You must complete a tax return when HMRC asks you to, even if no tax is due. You may have to pay a penalty if your tax return is late or you do not complete one when you should. The deadline depends on whether you complete a Company Tax Return (/prepare-file-annual-accounts-for-limited-company/overview) or a Self Assessment tax return (/self-assessment-tax-return-deadlines).
