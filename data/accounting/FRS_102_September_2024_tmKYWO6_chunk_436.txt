Credit risk For financial instruments within the scope of Section 11 or Section 12 to which the impairment requirements of IFRS 9 are not applied, a financial institution shall disclose by class of financial instrument: The amount that best represents its maximum exposure to credit risk at the end of the reporting period. This disclosure is not required for financial instruments whose carrying amount best represents the maximum exposure to credit risk. A description of collateral held as security and of other credit enhancements, and the extent to which these mitigate credit risk. The amount by which any related credit derivatives or similar instruments mitigate that maximum exposure to credit risk.
