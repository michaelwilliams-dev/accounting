The regulations may provide that a company does not comply with a relevant provision by keeping company records available for inspection at a place specified in the regulations unless conditions specified in the regulations are met. The regulations— need not specify a place in relation to each relevant provision; may specify more than one place in relation to a relevant provision. A requirement under a relevant provision to keep company records available for inspection is not complied with by keeping them available for inspection at a place specified in the regulations unless all the company's records subject to the requirement are kept there. Regulations under this section are subject to negative resolution procedure.
