If this requirement is not met in relation to a contract, then— in a case within subsection (1)(a), no purchase by the company of its own shares in pursuance of that contract may be made under this Chapter; in a case within subsection (1)(b), no such purchase following the variation may be made under this Chapter; in a case within subsection (1)(c), the purported release is void. Treatment of shares purchased Where a limited company makes a purchase of its own shares in accordance with this Chapter, then— if section 724 (treasury shares) applies, the shares may be held and dealt with in accordance with Chapter 6; if that section does not apply— the shares are treated as cancelled, and the amount of the company's issued share capital is diminished accordingly by the nominal value of the shares cancelled.
