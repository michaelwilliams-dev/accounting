Recovery of fees, charges or levy An amount payable by any person or body by virtue of section 957 or 958 is a debt due from that person or body to the Panel, and is recoverable accordingly. Miscellaneous and supplementary Panel as party to proceedings The Panel is capable (despite being an unincorporated body) of— bringing proceedings under this Chapter in its own name; bringing or defending any other proceedings in its own name. Exemption from liability in damages Neither the Panel, nor any person within subsection (2), is to be liable in damages for anything done (or omitted to be done) in, or in connection with, the discharge or purported discharge of the Panel's functions. A person is within this subsection if— he is (or is acting as) a member, officer or member of staff of the Panel, or he is a person authorised under section 947(5).
