Exceptions to right of pre-emption Exception to pre-emption right: bonus shares Section 561(1) (existing shareholders' right of pre-emption) does not apply in relation to the allotment of bonus shares. Exception to pre-emption right: issue for non-cash consideration Section 561(1) (existing shareholders' right of pre-emption) does not apply to a particular allotment of equity securities if these are, or are to be, wholly or partly paid up otherwise than in cash.
