(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Liability of officer in default 1121 Liability of officer in default This section has effect for the purposes of any provision of the Companies Acts to the effect that, in the event of contravention of an enactment in relation to a company, an offence is committed by every officer of the company who is in default. For this purpose “officer” includes— any director, manager or secretary, and any person who is to be treated as an officer of the company for the purposes of the provision in question.
