Neither the company nor, in a case within subsection (1)(d) or (e), the nominee or other shareholder may exercise any voting rights in respect of the shares. Any purported exercise of those rights is void. Notice of cancellation of shares Where a company cancels shares in order to comply with section 662, it must within one month after the shares are cancelled give notice to the registrar, specifying the shares cancelled. The notice must be accompanied by a statement of capital.
