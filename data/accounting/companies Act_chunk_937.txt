(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Notice requiring information: offences A person who— fails to comply with a notice under section 793 (notice requiring information about interests in company's shares), or in purported compliance with such a notice— makes a statement that he knows to be false in a material particular, or recklessly makes a statement that is false in a material particular, commits an offence. A person does not commit an offence under subsection (1)(a) if he proves that the requirement to give information was frivolous or vexatious.
