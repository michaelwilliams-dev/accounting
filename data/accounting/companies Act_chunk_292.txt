In this section— “former name” means a name by which the individual was formerly known for business purposes; “name” means the individual’s forename and surname. Where a secretary or joint secretary (or proposed secretary or joint secretary) is a peer or an individual usually known by a title, any requirement of this Act to provide the individual’s name because it forms part of the required information may be satisfied by providing that title instead of the individual’s forename and surname. The Secretary of State may by regulations— amend this section so as to change the required information about a secretary or joint secretary (or proposed secretary or joint secretary) who is an individual; repeal subsection (4).
