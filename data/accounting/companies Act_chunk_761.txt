Registration of resolution and supporting documents Within 15 days after the resolution for reducing share capital is passed the company must deliver to the registrar— a copy of the solvency statement, and a statement of capital. This is in addition to the copy of the resolution itself that is required to be delivered to the registrar under Chapter 3 of Part 3. The statement of capital must state with respect to the company's share capital as reduced by the resolution— the total number of shares of the company, the aggregate nominal value of those shares, [F956(ba) the aggregate amount (if any) unpaid on those shares (whether on account of their nominal value or by way of premium), and] for each class of shares— prescribed particulars of the rights attached to the shares, the total number of shares of that class, and the aggregate nominal value of shares of that class, F957...
