(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Chapter 1 Shares and share capital of a company Shares Shares In the Companies Acts“share”, in relation to a company, means share in the company's share capital. A company's shares may no longer be converted into stock. Stock created before the commencement of this Part may be reconverted into shares in accordance with section 620. In the Companies Acts— references to shares include stock except where a distinction between share and stock is express or implied, and references to a number of shares include an amount of stock where the context admits of the reference to shares being read as including stock. Nature of shares The shares or other interest of a member in a company are personal property
