If the additional changes do not result in the discontinuation of hedge accounting, an entity shall apply paragraph 12.25J. 12.25O Paragraphs 12.25P to 12.25V provide exceptions to the requirements specified in those paragraphs only. An entity shall apply all other hedge accounting requirements in this FRS, including the qualifying criteria in paragraph 12.18, to hedging relationships that were directly affected by interest rate benchmark reform. An entity shall also apply paragraph 12.20 (for a fair value hedge) or paragraph 12.23 (for a cash flow hedge) to account for any changes in the fair value of the hedged item or the hedging instrument.
