FRC | Professional Judgement Guidance 3 Mindset This section highlights five aspects of mindset that are especially relevant to exercising professional judgement in an effective manner. These mindset aspects are not only useful for longer, more considered judgement processes but also, when assimilated, can improve the quality of quicker and more intuitive judgements.
