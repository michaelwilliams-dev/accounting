In a cash flow hedge and net investment hedge, the lower of the following (in absolute amounts) shall be recognised in equity (in respect of cash flow hedges in the cash flow hedge reserve): the cumulative gain or loss on the hedging instrument from the date hedge accounting commenced to the reporting date of the last financial statements authorised for issue prior to 1 August 2014; and the cumulative change in fair value (ie the present value of the cumulative change of expected future cash flows) on the hedged item from the date hedge accounting commenced to the reporting date of the last financial statements authorised for issue prior to 1 August 2014.
