The amount by which a company's share capital is reduced under this section must not exceed 10% of the nominal value of the company's allotted share capital immediately after the reduction. A reduction of capital under this section does not extinguish or reduce any liability in respect of share capital not paid up. Nothing in Chapter 10 applies to a reduction of capital under this section.
