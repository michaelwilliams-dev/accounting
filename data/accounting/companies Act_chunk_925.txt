An election under this section is made by giving notice of election to the registrar. If the notice is given by subscribers wishing to form a private company— it must be given when the documents required to be delivered under section 9 are delivered to the registrar, and it must be accompanied by a statement confirming that no objection was received as mentioned in subsection (2). If the notice is given by the company, it must be accompanied by— a statement confirming that no objection was received as mentioned in subsection (2), and a statement containing all the information that is required to be contained in the company's PSC register as at the date of the notice in respect of matters that are current as at that date.
