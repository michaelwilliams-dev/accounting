Supplementary Application of provisions to companies not limited by shares In relation to a company other than a company limited by shares, the references in this Chapter to shares shall be read as references to the interest of its members as such, whatever the form of that interest. Application of provisions to nominees The provisions of this Chapter apply to a nominee acting on behalf of a subsidiary as to the subsidiary itself.
