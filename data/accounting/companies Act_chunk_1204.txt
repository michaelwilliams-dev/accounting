If at the date of restoration the company could not be registered under its former name without contravening section 66 (name not to be the same as another in the registrar's index of company names), it must be restored to the register— under another name specified— in the case of administrative restoration, in the application to the registrar, or in the case of restoration under a court order, in the court's order, or as if its registered number was also its name.
