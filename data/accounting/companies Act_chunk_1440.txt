(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes 1218 Exemption from liability for damages No person within subsection (2) is to be liable in damages for anything done or omitted in the discharge or purported discharge of functions to which this subsection applies. The persons within this subsection are— any recognised supervisory body, any officer or employee of a recognised supervisory body, and any member of the governing body of a recognised supervisory body.
