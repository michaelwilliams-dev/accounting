(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Effect of reduction of capital Liability of members following reduction of capital Where a company's share capital is reduced a member of the company (past or present) is not liable in respect of any share to any call or contribution exceeding in amount the difference (if any) between— the nominal amount of the share as notified to the registrar in the statement of capital delivered under section 644 [F966, 649, 1028A or 1032A of this Act or paragraph 7 of Schedule 4 to the Small Business, Enterprise and Employment Act 2015], and the amount paid on the share or the reduced amount (if any) which is deemed to have been paid on it, as the case may be. This is subject to section 653 (liability to creditor in case of omission from list). Nothing in this section affects the rights of the contributories among themselves.
