The company must— send a copy of a report under subsection (2)(b) to the registrar of companies, and if the report states that a second audit is needed, take such steps as are necessary for the carrying out of that audit. The company is guilty of an offence if— it fails to send a copy of a report under subsection (2)(b) to the registrar within the period of 21 days beginning with the date on which it receives it, in a case within subsection (6)(b), it fails to take the steps mentioned immediately it receives the report, or it has been convicted of a previous offence under this subsection and the failure to send a copy of the report, or take the steps, which led to the conviction continues after the conviction. A company guilty of an offence under this section is liable on summary conviction— in a case within subsection (5)(a) or (7)(a) or (b), to a fine not exceeding level 5 on the standard scale, and in a case within subsection (5)(b) or (7)(c), to a fine not exceeding [F1728one- tenth of level 5 on the standard scale][F1728one-tenth of the greater of £5,000 or level 4 on the standard scale] for each day on which the failure continues.
