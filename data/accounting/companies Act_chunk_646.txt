This power is exercisable only— by ordinary resolution at a meeting, and in accordance with section 511 (special notice of resolution to remove auditor). Nothing in this section is to be taken as depriving the person removed of compensation or damages payable to him in respect of the termination— of his appointment as auditor, or of any appointment terminating with that as auditor.
