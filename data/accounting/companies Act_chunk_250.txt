Chapter 8 Directors' residential addresses: protection from disclosure Protected information This Chapter makes provision for protecting, in the case of a company director who is an individual— information as to his usual residential address; the information that his service address is his usual residential address. That information is referred to in this Chapter as “protected information”. Information does not cease to be protected information on the individual ceasing to be a director of the company. References in this Chapter to a director include, to that extent, a former director.
