A person guilty of an offence under this section is liable— on conviction on indictment, to a fine; on summary conviction, to a fine not exceeding the statutory maximum.] Chapter 5 Directors' report Directors' report 415 Duty to prepare directors' report The directors of a company must prepare a directors' report for each financial year of the company. [F463(1A) Subsection (1) does not apply if the company qualifies as a micro-entity (see sections 384A and 384B).] For a financial year in which— the company is a parent company, and the directors of the company prepare group accounts, the directors' report must be a consolidated report (a “group directors' report”) relating to the undertakings included in the consolidation. A group directors' report may, where appropriate, give greater emphasis to the matters that are significant to the undertakings included in the consolidation, taken as a whole.
