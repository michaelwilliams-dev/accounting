In this section “specified” means specified in an order under this section. A description of charge may be specified, in particular, by reference to one or more of the following— the type of company by which it is created, the form of charge which it is, the description of assets over which it is granted, the length of the period between the date of its registration in the special register and the date of its creation. Provision may be made under this section relating to registers maintained under the law of a country or territory outside the United Kingdom. An order under this section is subject to negative resolution procedure.
