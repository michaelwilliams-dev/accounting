Part 29 Fraudulent trading Offence of fraudulent trading If any business of a company is carried on with intent to defraud creditors of the company or creditors of any other person, or for any fraudulent purpose, every person who is knowingly a party to the carrying on of the business in that manner commits an offence. This applies whether or not the company has been, or is in the course of being, wound up.
