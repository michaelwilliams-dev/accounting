For the purposes of subsection (4), where a person is present by proxy or proxies, he is treated as holding only the shares in respect of which those proxies are authorised to exercise voting rights. At a variation of class rights meeting, any holder of shares of the class in question present may demand a poll. For the purposes of this section— any amendment of a provision contained in a company's articles for the variation of the rights attached to a class of shares, or the insertion of any such provision into the articles, is itself to be treated as a variation of those rights, and references to the variation of rights attached to a class of shares include references to their abrogation.
