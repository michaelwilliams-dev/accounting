References in this section to delivery, in relation to a deed, include delivery as a deed where required. 859F Extension of period allowed for delivery Subsection (3) applies if the court is satisfied that— neither the company nor any other person interested in the charge has delivered to the registrar the documents required under section 859A or (as the case may be) 859B before the end of the period allowed for delivery under the section concerned, and the requirement in subsection (2) is met. The requirement is— that the failure to deliver those documents— was accidental or due to inadvertence or to some other sufficient cause, or is not of a nature to prejudice the position of creditors or shareholders of the company, or that on other grounds it is just and equitable to grant relief.
