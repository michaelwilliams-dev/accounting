Guidance Register a limited company as a subcontractor with payment under deduction by post Use postal form CIS305 to register as a Construction Industry Scheme (CIS) subcontractor with payment under deduction. From: HM Revenue & Customs (/government/organisations/hm-revenue-customs) Published 27 August 2024 Last updated 2 September 2024 — Before you start The quickest way to register as a CIS subcontractor (https://www.gov.uk/what-you-must- do-as-a-cis-subcontractor/how-to-register) is online. You may also be able to apply for g ross payment status (https://www.gov.uk/what-you-must-do-as-a-cis-subcontractor/gross- payment-status) if you do not want deductions to be made in advance by contractors. Register a limited company You can find information on completing the CIS305 postal form in the company registration guidance notes (https://assets.publishing.service.gov.uk/media/66d5b6fec52d5fb4c82ddd18/cis305-register- a-limited-company-as-a-subcontractor-notes.pdf) (PDF, 104 KB, 5 pages). Get all of your information together before you start. You will fill this form in online and you cannot save your progress. Fill in CIS305 form (https://www.tax.service.gov.uk/print-and- post/form/PersonalTax_A/1.0/CIS305_20167/cis305.xdp). Print and post it to HMRC, using the postal address shown on the form.
