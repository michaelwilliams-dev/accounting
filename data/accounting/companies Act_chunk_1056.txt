(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Expert's report (merger) An expert's report must be drawn up on behalf of each of the merging companies. The report required is a written report on the draft terms to the members of the company. The court may on the joint application of all the merging companies approve the appointment of a joint expert to draw up a single report on behalf of all those companies.
