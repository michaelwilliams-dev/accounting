(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Part 3 Amendments relating to investigations Companies Act 1985 (c. 6) The Companies Act 1985 has effect subject to the following amendments. After section 447 insert— “447A Information provided: evidence A statement made by a person in compliance with a requirement under section 447 may be used in evidence against him. But in criminal proceedings in which the person is charged with a relevant offence— no evidence relating to the statement may be adduced by or on behalf of the prosecution, and no question relating to it may be asked by or on behalf of the prosecution, unless evidence relating to it is adduced or a question relating to it is asked in the proceedings by or on behalf of that person. A relevant offence is any offence other than the following— an offence under section 451, an offence under section 5 of the Perjury Act 1911 (false statement made otherwise than on oath), or an offence under section 44(2) of the Criminal Law (Consolidation) (Scotland) Act 1995 (false statement made otherwise than on oath).” For section 449 substitute— “449 Provision for security of information obtained This section applies to information (in whatever form) obtained—
