Any requirement imposed by this Act to provide the required information about the members as joint secretaries (or proposed joint secretaries) may instead be satisfied by providing the information that would be required if the firm were a legal person and the firm had been appointed as secretary. 279M Secretary or joint secretary: offence of failure to notify of changes If a company fails, without reasonable excuse, to comply with section 279G, 279H or 279I, an offence is committed by— the company, and every officer of the company who is in default.
