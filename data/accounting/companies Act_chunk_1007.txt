This section does not apply to— a charge in favour of a landlord on a cash deposit given as a security in connection with the lease of land; a charge created by a member of Lloyd's (within the meaning of the Lloyd's Act 1982 F1156) to secure its obligations in connection with its underwriting business at Lloyd's; a charge excluded from the application of this section by or under any other Act. In this Part— “cash” includes foreign currency, “charge” includes— a mortgage; a standard security, assignation in security, and any other right in security constituted under the law of Scotland, including any heritable security, but not including a pledge, and “company” means a UK-registered company.
