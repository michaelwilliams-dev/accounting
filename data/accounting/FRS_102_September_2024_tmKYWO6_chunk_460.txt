For the purposes of this paragraph, oil and gas assets comprise only those assets used in the exploration, evaluation, development or production of oil and gas. [Deleted] Decommissioning liabilities included in the cost of property, plant and equipment Paragraph 17.10(c) states that the cost of an item of property, plant and equipment includes the initial estimate of the costs of dismantling and removing the item and restoring the site on which it is located, the obligation for which an entity incurs either when the item is acquired or as a consequence of having used the item during a particular period for purposes other than to produce inventories during that period. A first-time adopter may elect to measure this component of the cost of an item of property, plant and equipment at the date of transition to this FRS, rather than on the date(s) when the obligation initially arose. (lA) Decommissioning liabilities included in the cost of right-of-use assets Paragraph 20.47(d) states that the cost of a right-of-use asset includes the initial estimate of the costs of dismantling and removing the underlying asset and restoring the site on which it is located. A first-time adopter may elect to measure this component of the cost of a right-of-use asset at the date of transition to this FRS, rather than on the date(s) when the obligation initially arose.
