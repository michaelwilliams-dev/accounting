FRS 101 Reduced Disclosure Framework Disclosure exemptions from adopted IFRS for qualifying entities Objective The objective of this Financial Reporting Standard (FRS) is to set out the disclosure exemptions (a reduced disclosure framework) for the individual financial statements of subsidiaries, including intermediate parents, and ultimate parents that otherwise apply the recognition, measurement and disclosure requirements of adopted IFRS.
