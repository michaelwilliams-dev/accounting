B2.2 The purpose of Section 2 is to assist preparers in developing accounting policies for a transaction, other event or condition that is not specifically addressed by FRS 102. In the event of an inconsistency with another section of FRS 102, that section takes precedence. As a result, the changes to Section 2 are not expected to result in significant changes to existing practice. However, the updated concepts and pervasive principles will provide a basis for future standard-setting, and for the future development by preparers of accounting policies for a transaction, other event or condition that is not specifically addressed by FRS 102.
