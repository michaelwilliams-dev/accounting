(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes In the case of joint holders of shares or stock in a company, the company's register of members must state the names of each joint holder. In other respects joint holders are regarded for the purposes of this Chapter as a single member (so that the register must show a single [F101service] address). In the case of a company that does not have a share capital but has more than one class of members, there must be entered in the register [F102, with the names and addresses of the members,] a statement of the class to which each member belongs.
