scale. 790LK Power of court to order company to remedy defaults or delay Where a company makes default in complying with section 790LA, 790LC, 790LD, 790LE, 790LF, 790LG or 790LH, or regulations under section 790LI, an application may be made to the court for an order requiring the company to deliver to the registrar the information (or statements) necessary to rectify the position. The application may be made by— any person aggrieved by the default, any member of the company, or any person who is a registrable person or a registrable relevant legal entity in relation to the company.
