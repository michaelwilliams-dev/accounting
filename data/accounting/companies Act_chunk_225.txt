(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Payment in connection with share transfer: requirement of members' approval No payment for loss of office may be made by any person to a director of a company in connection with a transfer of shares in the company, or in a subsidiary of the company, resulting from a takeover bid unless the payment has been approved by a resolution of the relevant shareholders. The relevant shareholders are the holders of the shares to which the bid relates and any holders of shares of the same class as any of those shares.
