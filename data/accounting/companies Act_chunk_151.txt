(See end of Document for details) View outstanding changes Appointment [F152156AEach director to be a natural person A person may not be appointed a director of a company unless the person is a natural person. Subsection (1) does not prohibit the holding of the office of director by a natural person as a corporation sole or otherwise by virtue of an office. An appointment made in contravention of this section is void. Nothing in this section affects any liability of a person under any provision of the Companies Acts or any other enactment if the person— purports to act as director, or acts as shadow director, although the person could not, by virtue of this section, be validly appointed as a director. This section has effect subject to section 156B (power to provide for exceptions from requirement that each director be a natural person).
