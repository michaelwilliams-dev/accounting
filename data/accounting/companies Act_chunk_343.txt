[F270340ATraded companies: duty to circulate members' matters for AGM A company that is required under section 338A to include any matter in the business to be dealt with at an annual general meeting must— give notice of it to each member of the company entitled to receive notice of the annual general meeting— in the same manner as notice of the meeting, and at the same time as, or as soon as reasonably practicable after, it gives notice of the meeting, and publish it on the same website as that on which the company published the information required by section 311A.
