In this section “relevant creditor” means— a creditor in respect of a moratorium debt, or a creditor in respect of a priority pre-moratorium debt. The relevant creditors may not participate in the meeting summoned under section 901C. For the purposes of section 901D (statement to be circulated or made available)— the requirement in section 901D(1)(a) is to be read as including a requirement to send each relevant creditor a statement complying with section 901D; any reference to creditors entitled to attend the meeting summoned under section 901C includes a reference to relevant creditors.
