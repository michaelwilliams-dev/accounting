The statement required by this section is a statement confirming that person’s identity is verified (see section 1110A). A statement required by this section must be delivered within the period of 14 days beginning with the appointed day. But the registrar may by direction in writing extend that period by up to 14 days at a time. In this section— “the appointed day” means such day as the Secretary of State may by regulations appoint for the purposes of this section; “registrable person” does not include a person mentioned in section 790C(12)(a) to (d).
