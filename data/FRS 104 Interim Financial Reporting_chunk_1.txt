Home Library FRS 104 Interim Financial Reporting This FRS is intended for use in the preparation of interim reports by entities that apply FRS 102 The Financial Reporting Standard applicable in the UK and Republic of Ireland when preparing their annual financial statements. Entities applying FRS 101 Reduced Disclosure Framework to prepare annual financial statements may also use FRS 104 as a basis for their interim financial statements. The Table of Differences describes the relationships between UK and Ireland financial reporting standards and IFRS Accounting Standards.
