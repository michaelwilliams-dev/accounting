This is in addition to the right of the company to which the liability is owed to bring proceedings itself to enforce the liability. An “authorised group” of members of a company means— the holders of not less than 5% in nominal value of the company's issued share capital, if the company is not limited by shares, not less than 5% of its members, or not less than 50 of the company's members. The right to bring proceedings under this section is subject to the provisions of section 371. Nothing in this section affects any right of a member of a company to bring or continue proceedings under Part 11 (derivative claims or proceedings).
