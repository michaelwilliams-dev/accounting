Part 2 Communications in hard copy form Introduction A document or information is validly sent or supplied to a company if it is sent or supplied in hard copy form in accordance with this Part of this Schedule. Method of communication in hard copy form (1) A document or information in hard copy form may be sent or supplied by hand or by post to an address (in accordance with paragraph 4). For the purposes of this Schedule, a person sends a document or information by post if he posts a prepaid envelope containing the document or information.
