On making an order revoking a recognition order in respect of a body the Secretary of State must— give written notice of the making of the order to the body, take such steps as he considers reasonably practicable for bringing the making of the order to the attention of the members of the body, and publish a notice of the making of the order in such manner as he thinks appropriate for bringing it to the attention of any other persons who are in his opinion likely to be affected.
