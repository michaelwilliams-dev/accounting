The company must give the person a notice requiring the person— to inform the company whether the person has ceased to be a registrable person or a registrable relevant legal entity in relation to the company, and if the person has, to inform the company of the date on which the person so ceased. The notice must require the person to whom it is given to comply with the notice by no later than the end of the period of one month beginning with the day on which it is given.
