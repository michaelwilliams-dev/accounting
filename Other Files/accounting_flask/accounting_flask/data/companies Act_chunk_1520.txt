Rules under this section are referred to in this Part as “transparency rules”. Nothing in sections 89B to 89G affects the generality of the power to make rules under this section. 89B Provision of voteholder information Transparency rules may make provision for voteholder information in respect of voting shares to be notified, in circumstances specified in the rules— to the issuer, or to the public, or to both. Transparency rules may make provision for voteholder information notified to the issuer to be notified at the same time to the competent authority. In this Part “voteholder information” in respect of voting shares means information relating to the proportion of voting rights held by a person in respect of the shares.
