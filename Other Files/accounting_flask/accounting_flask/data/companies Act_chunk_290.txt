A notice under subsection (1)(a) must contain— a statement of the required information about the secretary or joint secretary (see sections 279J and 279K), and a statement by the company that the person has consented to act in that capacity. Subsection (1)(a) does not require a company, on its incorporation, to give notice in relation to a person named as the proposed secretary or one of the proposed joint secretaries of the company in the statement under section 12. A notice under this section must be given within the period of 14 days beginning with the day on which the person becomes or ceases to be the secretary or a joint secretary. 279H Duty to notify registrar of changes of information A company must give notice to the registrar of any change in the required information about the secretary or one of the joint secretaries of the company (see sections 279J and 279K). The notice must specify the date on which the change occurred. A notice under this section must be given within the period of 14 days beginning with the day on which the change occurs. 279I Notification of changes occurring before company’s incorporation A company must give notice to the registrar if— a person named in the statement under section 12 as the proposed secretary of the company did not become the secretary on its incorporation, or a person named in the statement under section 12 as one of the proposed joint secretaries of the company become did not become one of the joint secretaries on its incorporation.
