In the case of a company not having a share capital, copies need not be sent to anyone who is not entitled to receive notices of general meetings of the company. Where copies are sent out over a period of days, references in the Companies Acts to the day on which copies are sent out shall be read as references to the last day of that period. This section has effect subject to section 426 (option to provide [F480strategic report with supplementary material]).
