A requirement under this section may specify— the form and manner in which the information is to be provided; the period within which it is to be provided. The registrar may by notice in writing extend a period specified in a requirement under this section. 1092B Offence relating to provision of information A person who, without reasonable excuse, fails to comply with a requirement under section 1092A commits an offence.
