Penalty for contravention of this Chapter If a company contravenes any of the provisions of this Chapter, an offence is committed by— the company, and every officer of the company who is in default. A person guilty of an offence under this section is liable— on conviction on indictment, to a fine; on summary conviction, to a fine not exceeding the statutory maximum. Enforceability of undertakings to do work etc An undertaking given by any person, in or in connection with payment for shares in a company, to do work or perform services or to do any other thing, if it is enforceable by the company apart from this Chapter, is so enforceable notwithstanding that there has been a contravention in relation to it of a provision of this Chapter or Chapter 6. This is without prejudice to section 589 (power of court to grant relief etc in respect of liabilities). The appropriate rate of interest For the purposes of this Chapter the “appropriate rate” of interest is 5% per annum or such other rate as may be specified by order made by the Secretary of State.
