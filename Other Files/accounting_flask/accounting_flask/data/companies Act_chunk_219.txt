In the case of a transaction or arrangement entered into by a company in contravention of section 200, 201 or 203 with a person connected with a director of the company or of its holding company, that director is not liable by virtue of subsection (4)(c) if he shows that he took all reasonable steps to secure the company's compliance with the section concerned. In any case— a person so connected is not liable by virtue of subsection (4)(b), and a director is not liable by virtue of subsection (4)(d), if he shows that, at the time the transaction or arrangement was entered into, he did not know the relevant circumstances constituting the contravention. Nothing in this section shall be read as excluding the operation of any other enactment or rule of law by virtue of which the transaction or arrangement may be called in question or any liability to the company may arise.
