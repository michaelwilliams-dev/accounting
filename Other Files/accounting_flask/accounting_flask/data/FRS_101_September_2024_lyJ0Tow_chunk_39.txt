Part 4 of the regulations does not require qualifying partnerships to comply with the requirement in Part 6 of the Companies Act 2014 to prepare financial statements in accordance with applicable accounting standards. However, Part 4 of those regulations does require qualifying partnerships to comply with the requirement in Part 6 of the Companies Act 2014 to prepare financial statements that give a true and fair view of the assets, liabilities and financial position as at the financial year end date and of the profit or loss for the financial year. This FRS applies to financial statements intended to give a true and fair view.
