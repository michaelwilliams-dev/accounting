Part 42 Statutory Auditors Chapter 1 Introductory 1209 Main purposes of Part The main purposes of this Part are— to secure that only persons who are properly supervised and appropriately qualified are appointed as statutory auditors, and to secure that audits by persons so appointed are carried out properly, with integrity and with a proper degree of independence.
