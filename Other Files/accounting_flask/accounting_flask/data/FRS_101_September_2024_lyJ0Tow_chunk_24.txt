[…] A2.7 A qualifying entity that has financial instruments measured at fair value in accordance with the requirements of paragraph 36(4) of Schedule 1 to the Regulations (or equivalent8), is legally required to provide the relevant disclosures set out in UK-adopted international accounting standards. Such disclosures should be based on extant standards.
