For the purposes of subsection (1) it is irrelevant whether the person has, or has had, a relevant relationship with both companies at the same time. For the purposes of this section a person has a “relevant relationship” with a company if the person is— an officer, or a member or former member. If a company uses a name in contravention of this section an offence is committed by— the company, and every officer of the company who is in default. A person guilty of an offence under this section is liable on summary conviction to a fine not exceeding level 3 on the standard scale and, for continued contravention, a daily default fine not exceeding one-tenth of level 3 on the standard scale.
