International tax reform – Pillar Two model rules In July 2023 amendments were made to this FRS to insert sub-paragraph 8(iZA) to make an exemption available from some of the disclosures introduced by International Tax Reform—Pillar Two Model Rules (Amendments to IAS 12). Periodic Review 2024 In March 2024 amendments were made to this FRS as a result of the Periodic Review 2024. An entity shall apply the amendments to this FRS, as set out in Amendments to FRS 102 The Financial Reporting Standard applicable in the UK and Republic of Ireland and other FRSs – Periodic Review 2024, for periods beginning on or after 1 January 2026. Early application is permitted, provided that all the amendments are applied at the same time. If an entity applies these amendments before 1 January 2026 it shall disclose that fact.
